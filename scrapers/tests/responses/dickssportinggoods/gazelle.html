Buying Guide


Upgrade your fitness routine with the Gazelle Supreme Glider. Constructed of 2.25" diameter steel, this durable glider is the next level of both aerobic and resistance training. The Supreme delivers 10 challenging workouts in one with Power Pistons-hydraulic resistance, while Soft Glide technology minimizes stress on your joints. View instant feedback with the Supreme's grip pulse handlebars that track your pulse rate, calories, speed and more.

FEATURES:

  • Durable, steel glider with power pistons for more challenging aerobic and resistance training
  • Features patented, dual-action suspension for greater range of motion
  • Soft Glide technology for low-impact workout and less pressure on joints
  • Ideal for all fitness levels from beginner to advanced
  • Offers 10 different workouts within one workout
  • Grip pulse handlebars to track pulse rate
  • Extra-wide, non-skid foot platforms with rubber inserts for shock absorbance and stability
  • Five-function workout computer with feedback functions (speed, distance, time, calories burned, pulse rate)
  • Includes 3 Workout DVDs (Total Body Workout, Tony Little's Quick Shape Workout and Tony Little's Ultimate Body and Mind Workout)
  • Nutrition Guide to jump start healthy lifestyle
